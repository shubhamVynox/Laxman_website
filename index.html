Father

Mr. Channaram Suthar

Founded under the visionary leadership of Mr. Channaram Suthar, a master craftsman with deep roots in the rich carpentry heritage of Rajasthan. His dedication to quality and traditional craftsmanship laid a strong foundation for what would evolve into a legacy of excellence.

Son

Mr. Laxman Suthar

The journey continued with his elder son, Mr. Laxman Suthar, an accomplished Interior Designer, who expanded the family’s expertise into a comprehensive interior design and contracting firm. Under his guidance, the business grew beyond its traditional roots, embracing modern design sensibilities while maintaining a strong commitment to craftsmanship.

Today, CR's Interior Turnkey Solution is proud to be a team of 180+ skilled professionals, having successfully delivered 300+ projects across India. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and client satisfaction has positioned us as a trusted name in the industry.
